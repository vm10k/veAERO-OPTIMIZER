Overview

This tool is a specialized automation bot for Aerodrome Finance on the Base network. It optimizes your voting power to maximize weekly rewards.

  • Auto-Voting: Scans all pools to find the highest APR opportunities automatically.
  • Smart Diversification: Spreads votes across multiple high-yield pools to reduce risk.
  • Rebase & Compound: Automatically claims weekly inflation protection and locks it back to grow your veNFT.
  • Reward Swapping: Aggregates all bribing tokens and swaps them to USDC (or AERO) in one click.

How to Use

1
Connect & Configure Go to the Settings tab. Enter your veNFT ID (or comma-separated IDs). If you want to use the Auto-Voter, enter your private key (stored locally only).
2
Scan Pools Go to the Pools tab. Wait for the initial scan. The bot fetches live bribes and fees from the blockchain to calculate real APRs.
3
Activate Auto-Pilot In Settings, scroll to "Auto-Compound". Set your interval (e.g., 24 hours) and click "Start". The bot will now sleep and wake up to claim rewards and rebase automatically.
